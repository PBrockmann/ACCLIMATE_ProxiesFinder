Director of project : Claire Waelbroeck
Research Director at CNRS/LSCE.
Study of past climate variability and ocean circulation, based on the acquisition and interpretation of deep-sea sediment records. This includes measuring and interpreting the oxygen and carbon isotopic ratio, and trace element content of planktonic and benthic foraminifera, as well as developing chronologies common to ice cores and deep-sea climate records.

Data gathering and processing: Natalia Vazquez Riveiros

Data and web engineering : Patrick Brockmann
Data scientist at CEA/DSM/LSCE, member of the "CalculS" group of LSCE.
Research interests: data visualization, geo-spatial web applications, geo-services architectures and data processing in high performance computing environments.
